# 🐾 Meet Woof

Welcome to **Meet Woof** – The ultimate social and dating platform built specifically for dogs and their humans! 

Whether you're looking for a playdate at the local park, a walking buddy, or just want to connect with other dog parents in your area, Meet Woof provides a fun, intuitive, and safe environment to make those furry connections happen.

![Meet Woof Welcome](screenshots/welcome.jpg)

# ✨ Key Features

* **🐶 Dual Profiles:** Create and manage distinct profiles for both the human (owner) and the dog. Add essential details like breed, age, and energy level.
* **📍 Location & Filters:** Pinpoint your favorite dog parks on the map and use smart filters (distance, age range, gender, energy level) to find the perfect match.
* **❤️ Swipe & Match:** Browse through local dog profiles, swipe right to like, and get notified when there's a mutual match!
* **💬 Real-Time Chat:** Once matched, owners can instantly chat, share plans, and arrange playdates securely within the app.
* **⭐ Community Reviews:** Leave and read reviews about other dogs (e.g., "Great with small dogs!", "A bit too energetic") to ensure safe and compatible playdates.
* **🍖 Magic Treat Bowl (Powered by AI):** A unique daily feature that analyzes your dog's matching and swiping stats to generate a personalized, funny, and encouraging AI-driven motivation quote using **Google Gemini AI**.

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Language:** Kotlin
* **Architecture & UI:** Android SDK, XML Layouts, Material Design
* **Backend & Database:** Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore
* **AI Integration:** Google Gemini API (Gemini 2.5 Flash) via OkHttp
* **Image Loading:** Glide
